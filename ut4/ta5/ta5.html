The Naive Bayes model was trained and applied to the Golf dataset in different contexts, both with and without prior discretization of numerical data.

Obtained Data:

Accuracy without Discretization Accuracy with Discretization
Precision 66.67% 77.78%
Recall 64.29% 71.43%

There is greater confidence in the data when applying discretization. This is because the model assumes a Gaussian distribution of the data, which does not hold true for this dataset, making the model less precise.
